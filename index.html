welcome to miseryconfusion

the website of ✉️ 'nohup'

I am a DJ, producer, multimedia artist, and tool builder living in Seattle, WA. I operate the record label `illegal afters tracks` and am a co-founder of Ground Hum music festival. I also build Bandcamp Tempo Adjust, a tool for DJs to detect and adjust the tempo of music on Bandcamp.

You may also know me as 'sighup', DJ Kaaba Emoji, Wicked Lover and other nicknames.

You can find me as miseryconfusion on most social media platforms. For example: Soundcloud, Instagram, Bluesky, and Mastodon
