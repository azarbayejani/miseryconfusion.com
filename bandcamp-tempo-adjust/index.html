Bandcamp Tempo Adjust

Bandcamp Tempo Adjust is a web extension that allows DJs to detect and adjust tempo on Bandcamp.

Full feature list

  • BPM adjustment: available on album, track, and collection pages
  • BPM Detection: available on album and track pages
  • Purchase export: export your purchases with currency conversion to analyze your spending on Bandcamp
  • Master tempo: adjust track tempo without changing the pitch (available on album and track pages)
